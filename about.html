About us

From marketing agency to Technology Growth Partner

How we started, and who's behind it.

Tycoon Clap began in 2022 as a marketing agency, running paid campaigns and social growth for early clients. That work exposed a bigger gap: clients didn't need another vendor for their app, and another for their dashboards — they needed one team that could build the product, acquire the customers, and prove it was working. So we grew into a Technology Growth Partner, covering Software Engineering, Growth Marketing, and Data & AI as one connected system.
